About

Defend Together is a co-op experience that combines tower defense and PVE combat. Strategize with a friend or solo to survive and keep your tower in one piece from countless waves of various enemies. Inspired by tower defense games like BloonsTD and survival games like Zombie Estate, Defend Together tests your ability to make calculated decisions of fortification, combat, and finance in incrementally challenging waves. Like many classic co-op games, players can share one PC and one keyboard to join forces.

Team Reflection

As a team, we were able to rapidly polish the visuals of our game so that it went from looking like a barebones prototype to a well-developed game with consistent fantasy elements and a periodical setting. Our first prototype, alpha, and beta, were emphasized on the internal game mechanics, whereas now, our game has a fleshed out environment, UIs, item icons, and characters. By comparing our beta to our final version, the difference is like night and day. If we had 2 more weeks to work on our game, our highest priority would be:

  • Adding more defense/weapon variation and unlockables
  • Creating a shop mechanic that will help the player progress through higher waves
  • Polishing current mechanics such as enemy behavior and defense placement
